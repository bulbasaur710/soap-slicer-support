## FAQ

**Q: How do I cut the soap?**
Swipe across the soap to score grid lines, then tap blocks to cut them away.

**Q: How do I get a new soap?**
Tap or swipe anywhere after completing a soap, or tap the New Soap button.

**Q: How do I change colors?**
Use the color palette at the bottom of the screen.
